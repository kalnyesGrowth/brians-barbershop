Grooming Insights

Haircare

How Often Should You Get a Haircut?

For most men, every 3 to 4 weeks keeps a clean look. Military personnel and anyone with a skin fade should come in every 2 weeks to maintain crisp edges. If you are growing it out, 6 weeks is the max before shape is lost.

Grooming

Why a Hot Towel Makes the Difference

A hot towel opens your pores and softens facial hair before a shave. It reduces irritation, prevents ingrown hairs, and leaves your skin smoother for days. It is the reason a barbershop shave beats your bathroom mirror every time.

Reference

Show Your Barber a Photo

The fastest way to get the cut you want is to show a picture. Save a few reference photos on your phone from different angles. Your barber can adapt the style to your hair type and face shape. No guessing, no miscommunication.
