Who We Are

More than a haircut

Brian and Kim run a no-frills barbershop on Walpole Street in Stafford, VA. No gimmicks, no upsells, no appointment apps. Just two skilled barbers who take the time to get your cut right.

Show Brian a picture and he recreates what he sees. Every haircut comes with a complimentary hot towel for your face and neck, plus a clean back-of-neck shave. All for $20.

The wait can run long with only two chairs, but the regulars will tell you the same thing: it is worth it.

Your Experience

How it works

No appointments. No online booking. Walk in, sit down, walk out looking sharp.

Step 01
Walk In
Come in during business hours. Grab a seat in the waiting area. First come, first served.
Step 02
Tell Us What You Want
Describe your cut or show a picture on your phone. Brian and Kim can work from any reference.
Step 03
Get the Full Treatment
Your haircut includes a hot towel on the face and neck, a clean razor shave on the back of your neck, and a final check.
Step 04
Pay and Go
$20 for adults. Cash and cards accepted. No hidden fees, no product pressure. See you in 3 weeks.
